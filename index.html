About Us

SUP.VC is a corporate innovation company. This company plays an important role in the organization of events and implementation of the projects in Azerbaijan. It also supports the startup ecosystem. The company helps startups to get an investment, provide mentoring, customer support and product development.

SUP.VC cooperates with international brands such as Amazon Web Services, Slack, Techstars Startup Weekend, Founders Institute and more.

Why SUP.VC?

INNOVATION HUB

SUP VC is your gateway to innovation, serving as a dynamic hub for corporate innovation, project execution, and robust support for the startup ecosystem.

GLOBAL NETWORK

With extensive connections and collaborations with international brands like Amazon Web Services, Slack, Techstars Startup Weekend, and Founders Institute, SUP VC provides a global network that enriches opportunities for our community.

ENTREPRENEURIAL ECOSYSTEM SUPPORT

SUP VC is dedicated to supporting the startup ecosystem, offering holistic assistance. From mentorship and investment connections to strategic PR and brand positioning, we empower emerging ventures for success.

COLLABORATIVE WORKSPACES

Access 24/7 state-of-the-art office spaces that foster collaboration. Join SUP VC to connect with bright minds, creative individuals, and expand your professional network.

EDUCATIONAL INITIATIVES

Engage with SUP VC's educational initiatives, including programs like 'INNOSTART,' to promote innovation, knowledge-sharing, and the development of young talents.

EVENTS AND INITIATIVES

Explore the diverse events and initiatives hosted by SUP VC, from hackathons like STEAM and Cultech to workshops and programs, creating an environment where ideas flourish and connections thrive.

STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS

By choosing SUP VC, you enter into strategic partnerships that go beyond individual projects. Benefit from our expertise in project execution, corporate innovation, and continuous support for your growth.

COMMUNITY STRENGTH

Our community is the backbone of SUP VC. Connect with startups, international and local mentors, coaches, and our dedicated operating team, creating a powerful ecosystem for collaboration and growth.
